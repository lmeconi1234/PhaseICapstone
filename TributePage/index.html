BRUCE LEE

Hong Kong-American and Martial Artist

Here's a time line of Bruce Lee's life:

    1940 - Bruce Lee born at Jackson Street Hospital, San Francisco CA

    1941 - Appears in his first film "Golden Gate Girl"

    1941 - Moves to Hong Kong with his parents

    1946 - First "real" acting role in "The Birth of Mankind"

    1959 - Returns to the United States and enrolls in the Edison Technical School in Seattle, WA

    1960 - Graduates from technical school

    1961 - Accepts challenge from Karate Black Belt named Uechi. Defeats him at the YMCA in 11 seconds.

    1964 - Establishes a kung fu school in Oakland, CA

    1964 - Marries Linda Emery

    1965 - Son Brandon Lee is born

    1966 - Signs acting contract, moves to LA

    1970 - Returns to Hong Kong with son Brandon

    1972 - "Fist of Fury" premieres and Bruce moves to Rome to shoot "Way of the Dragon"

    1973 - Dies unexpectedly at the age of 32. 25,000 people attend his funeral. "Enter the Dragon" premieres just a month after his untimely death.
