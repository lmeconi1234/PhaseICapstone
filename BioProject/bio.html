About Me

I was an American aviator who set many flying records and championed the advancement of women in aviation. I became the first woman to fly solo across the Atlantic Ocean. On July 2, 1937, I disappeared over the Pacific Ocean while attempting to become the first female pilot to circumnavigate the globe.
